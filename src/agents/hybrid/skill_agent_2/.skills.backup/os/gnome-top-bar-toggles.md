## Open the right top-bar menu (depends on GNOME/Ubuntu version)
- **Quick Settings menu**: click the **top-right system status area** (network/sound/battery).
- **Calendar/Notifications popover**: click the **date/time** in the top bar.

## Case 1: Set volume to 100% fast (Quick Settings)
1. Open **Quick Settings** (top-right system status area).
2. Find the **Volume** slider.
3. **Click the far-right end of the slider track** to jump to **100%** (faster than dragging).

If you don’t see a volume slider: open **Settings → Sound** (can be hidden if no audio device).

## Case 2: Toggle “Do Not Disturb”
### If it’s in Quick Settings (newer GNOME)
1. Open **Quick Settings**.
2. Toggle **Do Not Disturb**.

### If it’s in the Calendar/Notifications popover (common on Ubuntu/GNOME variants)
1. Click the **date/time** to open the popover.
2. Find **Do Not Disturb** (often near the bottom).
3. **Click the toggle switch itself** (row/label clicks can be unreliable).

### Fallback (always available)
- **Settings → Notifications → Do Not Disturb**.
