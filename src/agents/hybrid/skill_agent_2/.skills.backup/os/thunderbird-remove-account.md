## Remove an email account (and optionally local data)
1. Open **Thunderbird**.
2. Open **Account Settings**:
   - **Menu ≡ → Account Settings** (common), or
   - **Tools → Account Settings** (some versions).
3. In the left account list, click the account you want to delete.
4. Bottom-left, open **Account Actions ▾**.
5. Click **Remove Account…**.
6. In **Remove Account and Data**, confirm **Remove** (pick the option that removes local data if you also want messages/settings deleted from this computer).
7. Verify the account disappears from the left account list.
