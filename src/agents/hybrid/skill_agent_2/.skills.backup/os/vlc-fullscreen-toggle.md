## Toggle fullscreen (fast)
- Press **F** to toggle fullscreen on/off.

## Alternatives
- Menu: **View → Fullscreen**
- Often works: **double‑click** the video area

## If the shortcut doesn’t work
- Check/restore in **Tools → Preferences → Hotkeys** (fullscreen action).
